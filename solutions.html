Our Solutions

APT Solutions delivers secure, scalable, and process-driven examination systems and academic services designed for universities and autonomous institutions.

Our solutions are built to address the operational, security, and compliance challenges of higher education examinations. We support institutions across the complete examination lifecycle, combining technology with proven operational processes.

Concealed Evaluation System (Barcode-Based)

The Concealed Evaluation System through Barcode ensures complete anonymity of student identity during the evaluation process. Barcode-enabled answer books conceal all personal identifiers, eliminating bias and enhancing fairness.

  • Unique barcode stickers for each student and subject
  • Concealment of student identity from examiners
  • Centralized allocation of answer books
  • Secure marks processing and result generation
  • Easy retrieval and audit-ready records

Concealed Digital Evaluation System (Onscreen)

The Concealed Digital Evaluation System enables secure onscreen evaluation of scanned answer books using a controlled, desktop-based application.

  • Desktop-based evaluator software
  • Secure login with role-based access
  • Automatic totaling and validation of marks
  • Support for optional and maximum mark rules
  • Optional dashboards for authorized authorities

Pre-Examination & Post-Examination Services

We provide comprehensive operational support for traditional pen-and-paper based examinations, ensuring accuracy, coordination, and timely delivery.

  • Student data compilation (physical and online)
  • Exam form processing and validation
  • Seating arrangement and block-wise allocation
  • Marks processing and result preparation
  • Marksheet and certificate printing

OMR Solutions

Our OMR services support large-scale examinations with accurate processing, validation, and result generation.

  • OMR sheet supply
  • High-speed OMR scanning and processing
  • Error validation and data verification
  • Result generation and reporting

Data Entry, Digitization & Archiving

We assist institutions in managing academic records through secure data entry, digitization, and long-term archival solutions.

  • Data entry services
  • Digitization of old records
  • Secure archiving and retrieval
  • Document management systems
