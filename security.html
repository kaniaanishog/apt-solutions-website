Security, Confidentiality & Compliance

Examination data demands the highest level of confidentiality, accuracy, and process integrity. Our systems are designed with security and compliance at their core.

APT Solutions follows a security-first approach across all examination systems and services. Our processes are aligned with internationally recognized standards such as ISO and CMMI, ensuring confidentiality, accountability, and audit readiness for universities and autonomous institutions.

Confidentiality of Student Information

Protecting student identity and examination data is a fundamental requirement of our solutions. Our concealed evaluation systems ensure that no personally identifiable student information is visible to examiners during the evaluation process.

  • Complete concealment of student identity during evaluation
  • Barcode-based anonymization of answer books
  • Controlled access to sensitive examination data
  • Strict handling procedures for physical and digital records

Access Control & Authorization

Our systems enforce role-based access to ensure that users can only access information relevant to their assigned responsibilities.

  • Role-based user access (administrator, examiner, moderator)
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Separation of duties across examination stages
  • Controlled access to marks, results, and reports

Data Integrity & Accuracy

Maintaining the accuracy and integrity of examination data is critical. Our systems are designed to eliminate manual errors and enforce validation at every stage.

  • Question-wise marks entry with validation rules
  • Automatic totaling and consistency checks
  • Prevention of exceeding maximum permissible marks
  • Secure storage of evaluation and result data

Auditability & Traceability

Our examination systems are designed to support institutional audits, inspections, and re-evaluation requirements.

  • Complete tracking of answer books using unique identifiers
  • Logs of evaluation and marks submission activities
  • Easy retrieval of physical and scanned answer books
  • Support for re-evaluation and verification processes

Deployment & Infrastructure Security

APT Solutions primarily delivers desktop-based applications to ensure controlled execution environments. Deployment models are designed to align with institutional IT policies.

  • On-premise and in-house server deployments
  • Restricted network access configurations
  • Optional web dashboards for authorized officials
  • Minimal dependency on external internet connectivity

Standards Alignment & Compliance

Our internal processes and systems are aligned with established quality and security standards, including ISO and CMMI frameworks. These standards guide our approach to information security, process maturity, and continuous improvement.

Detailed certification information is available on our Certifications page.
