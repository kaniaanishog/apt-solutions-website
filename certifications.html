Certifications & Standards

Our processes and systems are aligned with internationally recognized standards for quality, security, and process maturity.

APT Solutions follows structured quality and security frameworks to ensure reliable delivery of examination systems and services. These certifications reflect our commitment to process discipline, information security, and continuous improvement.

ISO 27001 – Information Security Management

ISO 27001 provides a framework for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an information security management system.

Alignment with ISO 27001 ensures that APT Solutions follows structured controls for protecting sensitive examination data, managing access, and mitigating information security risks.

ISO 9001 – Quality Management Systems

ISO 9001 focuses on consistent quality delivery and customer satisfaction through well-defined and repeatable processes.

Our alignment with ISO 9001 supports disciplined service delivery, documentation, and continuous process improvement across all examination-related services.

CMMI Level 5 – Process Maturity

CMMI Level 5 represents the highest level of process maturity, emphasizing optimization, performance measurement, and continuous improvement.

Our CMMI-aligned practices guide the design, development, deployment, and maintenance of examination systems, ensuring predictable outcomes and institutional reliability.

Certification details and supporting documentation can be provided to institutions upon formal request as part of evaluation, audit, or tender processes.
